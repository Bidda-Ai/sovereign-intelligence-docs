## Reporting a Vulnerability

Security and cryptographic integrity are the foundation of the Bidda protocol. 

If you discover a vulnerability in our L402 payment gateway, the Python deterministic auditor, or find a hallucination within a verified node's `actionable_schema`, **do not open a public GitHub issue.**

Please securely email our security team directly at **security@bidda.com**.

We will acknowledge your report within 12 hours and provide a timeline for the patch and subsequent node re-hashing.
